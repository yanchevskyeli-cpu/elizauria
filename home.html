Our Origin

The Boy Who Built a Country

A kid wanted to be known. Nothing worked, so he built his own country instead: a visa, a passport, an ID, a driver's license, one set for him and one for his brother.

One invitation email later, Elizauria had two citizens. Then a website. Then the world noticed, and a bedroom became one of the biggest nations on Earth. Now the story includes you.
